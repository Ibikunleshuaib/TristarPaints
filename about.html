Who we are

TriStar Paints & Allied Products Limited is a manufacturer and marketer of premium Decorative Paints, Marine, Industrial and High Performance Coatings, Auto refinishes, and Wood finishes, for the beautification and protection of physical assets and infrastructures.


The founders of TriStar Paints & Allied Products Limited combined vast experience from both local and international companies with a passion for home improvements, to make high quality products, and provide unequalled customer service. We are strategically positioned to revolutionize the industry through our guiding values of Quality, Integrity, Innovation, and Customer service. These, coupled with continuous pursuit of advancement, keep us in good position to serve our customers satisfactorily at all time.


Our vision is to be the leader in the paints and coatings manufacturing and marketing industry in Nigeria, and beyond. We also provide painting services through our well trained and courteous painters, to deliver a satisfactory job that will exceed your expectations.


Our factory is currently located at Km 14, Olorunda-Aba Road, Ibadan, Oyo State. We also have trade outlets spread all over the country to promptly deliver our products and services anywhere in Nigeria.


Our product portfolio is robust and continues to expand, with our research and development experts developing novel solutions to the needs of our customers at affordable prices.
